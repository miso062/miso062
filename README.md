## About

Hi! I'm a backend-leaning developer who enjoys turning messy operational problems into clear, maintainable software.
I care about readable structure, practical data flows, and products that make sense to both users and the teams who operate them.

#### On the web side

I have built intranet systems and internal tools across both backend and frontend, using Java, Spring, Node.js, REST APIs, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. I like working close to business workflows, where a usable interface and reliable data handling need to fit together.

#### On the data side

I have worked on EAI systems and mainly use relational databases such as MySQL, Oracle, and MSSQL. I enjoy schema thinking, CRUD flows, reporting queries, and tracing how data moves between systems.

#### On the workflow side

I have experience with service operations and workspace migration, including a Microsoft 365 to Google Workspace transition project. These days, I work on both development and planning for an account-management solution, so I often think about product requirements, permissions, user flows, and operational edge cases together.

#### Off the clock

I like writing, sewing, studying English conversation, and building small apps with Flutter or React Native. Many of my side projects start from daily-life ideas, including budgeting, recipes, and personal workflow helpers.
